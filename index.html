Some FAQs you need to know...

What is the OzSpell Challenge?

It is a new national spelling competition to be held annually and open to 10 - 14 year old students in all senior primary and junior high schools. That's more than 9500 schools! Over 1,080,000 Australian students are currently eligible to have a go. Awesome!

The competition ...

There are three stages in the OzSpell Challenge ...

1)  Classroom and School Competitions
2)  District Finals
3)  National Grand Final

The winner of each District Final in every state and territory of Australia will travel to the National Grand Final to be held at the Seymour Centre, the University of Sydney, in August 2012, where a national champion will be determined. The National Grand Final will be recorded and broadcast on Australian network television.

Heaps of prizes ...

Every student who participates is a winner. The closer you get to be a district finalist, the more stuff you can win. The champion of champions will receive FIVE THOUSAND DOLLARS cash, a huge trophy and another FIVE THOUSAND DOLLARS cash for their school's library resources. Wow! That could be you ... There's more ... you also get to appear on national television on the OzSpell Challenge program. Winners are grinners ... you do like to win, eh?

How it all works ...

Each eligible student may register soon for a fee of $8.80 on our interactive website and be issued with a secret password enabling access to the WordL*, a vocabulary booklet containing 2500 neat words specially chosen by Macquarie Dictionary as the principal words for the competition. All registered students will qualify for free access ($40.00 value ... yes, that's right, $40.00 value, free) to Macquarie Dictionary and Macquarie Thesaurus online for a year! What an amazing deal ... wonderful people at Macquarie Dictionary!
